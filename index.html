My favourite skateboard tricks

Hello , my name is Matt . One of my passions is skateboard that's why I will show you 5 of my favourite skateboard tricks . Since I'm afraid of ramps, it will be exclusively flat tricks !

5. Frontside shove-it

The frontside shove-it is just a 180 degrees rotation of the board , it's one of the basics but still looks cool !

4. Frontside 360

The frontside 360 is harder . You just have to do a 360 with the board , easy to say hard to do !

3. Hardflip

A frontside shove-it combined to a kickflip . As said in the name , this trick is hard but not impossible it just requires a year or two of practice !

2. Treflip

The combination of a 360 shove-it and a kickflip an intermediate tricks that looks very nice !

1. Frontside kickflip

Just have to do a 180 with the board and add a kickflip , not an advanced trick but always good looking !
